# Retail Performance & Profitability Analytics Suite

## 📊 Overview

An end-to-end retail analytics project designed to investigate declining profitability despite rising revenue. This project transforms raw transactional data into actionable insights through SQL-based data engineering and an interactive Tableau dashboard suite.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

The organization is experiencing increasing revenue but declining profitability. Leadership lacks visibility into the drivers of margin erosion across products, regions, customer segments, and sales channels.

This project was built to answer:

**Why is profitability declining despite revenue growth?**

* Tableau (interactive dashboard suite development)
* Data modeling (creation of unified analytical dataset)

---

## 📌 Key Insights

* Revenue and profitability are heavily concentrated in the North East region.
* Premium pricing tiers generate disproportionately higher margins.
* High discounting significantly erodes profitability.
* Certain high-revenue products contribute low profit margins.
* Online and corporate channels outperform other sales channels in revenue.
